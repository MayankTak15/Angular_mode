About Dr. Emily Johnson

Welcome to Our Practice

Dr. Emily Johnson is a board-certified pediatrician with over 15 years of experience in providing compassionate and comprehensive care to children of all ages.

Education and Training

  • MD from Harvard Medical School
  • Residency in Pediatrics at Boston Children's Hospital
  • Fellowship in Pediatric Infectious Diseases at Johns Hopkins Hospital

Specializations

  • General Pediatrics
  • Pediatric Infectious Diseases
  • Childhood Vaccinations
  • Developmental Assessments

Philosophy of Care

Dr. Johnson believes in a patient-centered approach, working closely with families to ensure the best possible health outcomes for every child. She is committed to staying at the forefront of pediatric medicine and providing evidence-based care.

Professional Memberships

  • American Academy of Pediatrics
  • Pediatric Infectious Diseases Society
  • American Medical Association
